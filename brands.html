How It Works

Define Audience and Goals

 

There's a lot Arteria can help you achieve. First, our brand partners identify what their goals are and who they want to target. Then we'll shape a campaign around that.

Review Results and OptimIze

 

The campaign doesn't have to end when the samples go out. We offer digital re-marketing features like Facebook Custom Audiences so you can keep marketing to the sample recipients and audience insights so you can gain valuable feedback.

Match with retail Channels

 

Arteria matches you with a retail partner that fits your goals and targeting. Our double opt-in process ensures that both you and the retail partner are happy with the placement.

Streamline Logistics

 

Brands bulk ship samples or inserts to the point of fulfillment, where our retail partners place them in the appropriate shipments. Arteria coordinates to ensure this all runs smoothly.
